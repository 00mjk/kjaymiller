[NEW!] Sorted Drafts Action Group

- None

My Friends over at StaySorted.com have released Sorted3 for iOS.

To celebrate, I put together a set of Draft Actions that you can use to import Drafts into your Sorted Workflow.

Adding Items

With Add to Sorted you can add an item to your inbox. If you select the paste variant you can paste your draft into the new task/event screen to take advantage of Sorted's natural language parsing for date selection. Lastly if you plan to do a lot of planning on a project you can make a list of items to be added in bulk.

Time to Review

Sometimes you need to review your plans while you are working on something new.

The Open Sorted action allows you to jump to Sorted directly from inside of Drafts. By default you go to the Today View, but there are variants to take you to the Inbox the Search Screen or a specific date that you can select beforehand.

All seven (7) of these actions are available individually and as a group in the Drafts Action Directory.
